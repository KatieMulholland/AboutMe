About Me

My name is Katie Mulholland and this is my about me page! I am 21 years old and I am a student at Coastal Carolina University. I am a senior and I study Digital Culture and Design with a minor in journalism. My interests include music production, social media, web design, and photography. I am inspired by photographers like Michael Kenna, Laura Zalenga, and Kyle Thompson. I am also inspired by images and graphics that I see throughout social media, including Instagram, Tik Tok, and Pinterest.

One aspiration I have for the future would be to work with a company that helps create digital media projects and advertisements for individuals and companies.

Throughout my time at Coastal Carolina, I have worked on a number of class projects that focused on web design, graphic design, coding using CSS and HTML, and advertising campaigns. This page itself was created for a class project!

In my photography work, I focus on simplicity and familiarity. In graphic design work, I focus on simple colors and designs. In this portfolio, I inlcuded vector art graphic designs.

I have learned how to work with the Adobe Creative Suite and grasped a proficient understanding of how these softwares work. All the photographs and designs in this portfolio have been edited or created using these softwares.

Skills I have learned throughout my college career include:

-Adobe Photoshop

-Adobe After Effects

-Adobe Illustrator

-HTML and CSS Coding language

-Web Design
